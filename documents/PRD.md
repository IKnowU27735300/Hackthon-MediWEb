# Product Requirement Document (PRD): MediWeb

## 1. Project Overview
**MediWeb** is an "Advanced Clinical Operations Ecosystem" designed to bridge the gap between healthcare providers, clinical staff, and medical resource suppliers. It transforms traditional, manual clinic management into a high-performance, automated, and visually stunning digital experience.

### 1.1 The Problem
Clinics often struggle with:
*   **Inventory Lag**: Doctors don't know if a resource is available until they need it.
*   **Fragmented Communication**: Requests to suppliers are often lost in emails or phone calls.
*   **Manual Tracking**: Staff spend hours reconciling stocks and booking appointments.
*   **Poor User Experience**: Older medical software is clunky, slow, and uninspiring.

### 1.2 The Solution
MediWeb provides a unified, real-time platform where:
*   **Doctors** can manage patients and request resources instantly.
*   **Suppliers** can fulfill orders with one click based on regional proximity.
*   **Assistants** can coordinate patient flows seamlessly.
*   **Patients** enjoy a modern, secure portal for bookings and communication.

---

## 2. Key Features

### 2.1 Clinical Operations (Doctor/Assistant)
*   **Intelligent Dashboard**: At-a-glance view of today's appointments, inventory alerts, and patient activity.
*   **Interactive Patient Reports**: A digital clinical note-taking area with real-time auto-save and resource integration.
*   **Smart Resource Requests**: Directly flag stock usage during a consultation. If a resource is low, doctors can trigger a fulfillment request to a local supplier.

### 2.2 Supply Chain Integration (Supplier)
*   **Regional Ecosystem**: Suppliers are automatically matched with clinics in their specific city/area.
*   **Fulfillment Dashboard**: A dedicated view for suppliers to track, accept, and dispatch medical supplies to clinics.
*   **Atomic Inventory Sync**: Stock levels are only updated when a supplier confirms dispatch, ensuring the "source of truth" is always accurate.

### 2.3 User Experience (UX)
*   **Premium Aesthetic**: Uses "Glassmorphism" (frosted glass effects) and vibrant, harmonious color palettes to reduce "software fatigue."
*   **Real-time Synchronization**: Powered by live data streams—no page refreshing required to see new orders or appointments.

---

## 3. Success Metrics
*   **Time Savings**: Reduce manual inventory reconciliation time by 80%.
*   **Fulfillment Accuracy**: Zero "phantom" stock deductions through the supplier confirmation workflow.
*   **Regional Efficiency**: 100% of resource requests routed to the nearest available supplier.

---

## 4. Future Roadmap
*   **AI Diagnostics**: Integration of LLMs to assist doctors in summarizing clinical findings.
*   **Mobile Companion**: Dedicated app for suppliers to scan QR codes for dispatch.
*   **Predictive Stocking**: AI-driven alerts that predict when a clinic will run out of resources based on historical patient volume.
